Enjoying Nature

Being amongst nature is a hobby of mine that I hold close to my heart. Sometimes I find it hard to distract myself from society and all the qualms that come with it, when that happens I find it best to just take a break and go on a walk through the woods or whatever non-human developed nature area I can find. It's so calming to hear nothing but the wind and birds chirping, it really just helps me seperate a bit from society and often has me leaving with a greater understanding of life through my peaceful thinking out there.


Gaining Higher Knowledge

Educating myself further has always been a goal of mine and has turnt into somewhat of a hobby. I believe in the idea that knowledge is power and believe that we as humans should be doing everything in our power to expand our knowledge. That being said at this point in life I can't do much but educate myself, school has been a major part of that but even outside of school I try to make learn as much as possible whether it comes from work or whatever I am doing. I hope whatever life throws me that this hobby is the one I can stick with as I believe it betters me as a human
